ABOUT US

Originating from Osaka city, Osaka Kuma is a popular Japanese beauty & personal care store that is famous amongst locals and tourists for its wide variety and reasonable prices. We have 7 outlets in Japan and 1 overseas branch in Hong Kong. In line with our mission to bring our business to the global scene by 2020, we have specially curated a selection of quality products for our Southeast Asian store opening in Singapore and on LAZADA, Shopee and Q10.

Our goal is to provide customers the Best Price, Faster Delivery, Hot-selling and Authentic products from Japan coupled with excellent customer service.
